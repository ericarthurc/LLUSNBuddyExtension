Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

Our Extension collects and processes the following types of information:

  • Search queries: When you use the search functionality provided by our Extension, your search queries are processed to provide you with search results.

  • Browser settings: The Extension modifies your default search engine settings to provide its functionality.

Usage Data

  • Usage Data is not collected when using the Service.

  • Searches are not logged by LLU SN Buddy.

  • LLU SN Buddy processes, but does not save, your searches into full URL strings to send to Loma Linda's ServiceNow instance.

Third-Party Services

Our Extension uses Loma Linda's ServiceNow instance to process search queries and provide search results. When you use our search functionality, your search queries are sent to Loma Linda's ServiceNow instance. Please refer to ServiceNow's corporation privacy policy.
